• IN MIND

    Exploring the development of a multimedia portrait.

    ILLUSTRATOR
    AFTER EFFECTS
    PREMIERE PRO

    MEDIA

    The goal for this project was to explore the theme of detatchment through media and collage. Experiments with footage, photographs and visual FX served as a starting point for the design of the portrait.

    PROFILE

    The structure of the portrait was defined by a series of shapes that would contain the media experiements. For this to work effectively, the shapes had to be simple enough in some areas to contain media and refined in other areas to retain the likeness of the individual.

    OUTCOME

    The result is a portrait that captures the physical appearance of the individual while using media to add depth their identity.
