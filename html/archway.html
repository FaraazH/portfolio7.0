• ARCHWAY

    Giving a new lease of life to artwork in the public domain.

    CINEMA 4D
    ILLUSTRATOR
    AFTER EFFECTS

    PUBLIC DOMAIN

    In 2017, The Met made 492,000 images of artwork in their collection available for free and unrestricted use. This project seeks to explore how we can use these images as inspiration for new compositions. The artwork in question is the Arch of Gallienus.

    NARRATIVE

    Developing a new composition involved structuring a narrative from which assets could be created. "Searching for connection" served as thematic inspiration from which the scene was structured.

    SCENE

    Modelling the scene involved striking a balance across camera angles, visual assets, lighting and colour.

    OUTCOME

    The result is a scene that uses artwork in the public domain as a source of inspiration for structuring a new composition.
