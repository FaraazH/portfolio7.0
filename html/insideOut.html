• INSIDE OUT

    Exploring life during the pandemic through music and animation.

    ILLUSTRATOR
    AFTER EFFECTS
    LOGIC PRO X

    CONNECTION

    This projects seeks to capture our desire connect while being physically disconnected to reality. Windows are used as a visual metaphor to capture this disparity.

    MESSAGE

    The song itself is the message; transmitted visually as a pulse across each scene. As the window opens, the connection is broken and the divide between being inside and outside is exposed.

    OUTCOME

    Inside out came runner up in Yorkshie Sculpture Parks, “Tuning into Nature” prize.
